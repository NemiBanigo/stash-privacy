Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: Stash stores your saved products locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. We don't collect, share, or sell any data about you.

What Stash stores

When you save a product, Stash stores the following information locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage:

  • Product title, price, and URL
  • Product image URL
  • Variant information (sizes, colours)
  • Collections and notes you create
  • Your price alert and tracking preferences

Where your data goes

Nowhere. All data is stored exclusively on your device using chrome.storage.local. It is never transmitted to any external server, never shared with third parties, and is not accessible to anyone other than you.

What Stash reads

Stash reads page content (product title, price, image, variants) only when you open the extension on a product page. It does not monitor your browsing history, track which pages you visit, or read pages in the background.

Price checking

If you enable price alerts, Stash periodically fetches the product pages you have already saved to check for price changes. These requests go directly from your browser to the store's website — there is no intermediary server. No data about the request is logged or stored outside your device.

Analytics & tracking

Stash contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or any form of third-party tracking.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a meaningful way, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. We won't reduce your privacy rights without clear notice.
